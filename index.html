Simple Present Tense – Grammar Lesson

We use the Simple Present Tense to talk about:

  • Habits → I wake up early.
  • Facts → The sun rises in the east.
  • Routines → She goes to school at 7:00 a.m.

Verb Forms:

  • I/You/We/They: play, eat, go
  • He/She/It: plays, eats, goes

Examples:

  • He eats breakfast.
  • They go to school.
